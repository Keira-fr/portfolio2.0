Meme Mashup

For this piece, I combined a few well known images and memes to create a new meme varriation. This meme in particular is meant to resemble the format of the infmaous Thousand Yard Stare meme, with L from Deathnote as the "soldier", and the image of vegtables being what he's reacting to.

The running joke is the L is addicted to sweets and we rarely see him eat anything healthy.

You can learn more about the original meme format at Know Your Meme!
